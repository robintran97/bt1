1. Formation through Boys Planet and other activities

Zerobaseone was formed through Mnet's reality competition show Boys Planet, which aired from February 2 to April 20, 2023. The show brought 98 contestants from around the world, primarily in South Korea, China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Japan, Thailand, Vietnam, the United States, and Canada, to compete and to debut in a multinational boy group.[3][4] Out of a pool of 98 contestants, only the top nine would make the final lineup.[5] All members were announced in the finale episode, which was broadcast live on April 20, 2023.[6]

2023: Debut with Youth in the Shade, and Melting Point

On June 22, the first episode of the group's pre-debut reality show Camp Zerobaseone was aired on Mnet. The reality show consists of 3 episodes aiming for a journey that will help the members bond and get to know one another even better.[16] On June 26, the album was reported to have amassed over 780,000 pre-orders within five days, surpassing 1.08 million copies by July 4,[17] setting the record for the highest number of pre-orders for a K-pop debut album.[18] The group officially debuted on July 10, with the EP Youth in the Shade,[19] and earned their first music show win on SBS M's The Show eight days after their debut.[20] In conjunction with their debut promotion, the group held their first fan concert at Gocheok Sky Dome on August 15 to an audience of 18,000.[21][22] Youth in the Shade subsequently sold more than 2 million copies, setting a sales record for a K-pop debut album.[23] On August 20, it was announced that Zerobaseone would be releasing their second EP in November.[24] On October 6, it was announced that the group's second EP Melting Point would be released on November 6.[25] On October 26, the album amassed over 1.7 million pre-orders[26] and reportedly sold over 2 million copies in its first week of release, making them the first K-pop group to do so in debut year.[27]
